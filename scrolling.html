ABOUT

Our flightcase is designed to be the perfect flightcase for your specific needs.
Product Details:

  • The case can carry up to 15 kg.
  • Material thickness 7mm
  • 22/22 mm Edge Protector (1.5 mm aluminium)
  • 7 mm Hybrid Lid Location, 1.5 mm aluminium
  • Ball Corner (small) 1.2 mm galvanised steel, cranked, 22 mm Edge Profile
  • Medium Corner Brace (1.2 mm galvanised steel)
  • Maximum empty weight 12,7kg

    • HISTORY

  • The Flightcase Shop is created with the purpose of fullfilling your needs for the perfect flightcase.
  • The case is created as a configurator to give the customer the freedom to design it with personal colors and preferable locks and handles.
  • The Flightcase Shop was founded in march 2021 as a schoolproject at Københavns Erhvervsakademi, but that doesn't mean that our webshop and product isn't created fully by love and passion.
  • It's has always been our dream to walk into the flightcase industry with new and more modern designs and we are very exited that we finally were able to pursue our big dream.
  • It has been a long ride, but we are happy to be here!
